Welcome to MakerSpace!

Are you a cosplayer? Or maybe you just like crafting? Either way this is the website for you! Here at Maker Space we specialise in providing cheap materials with world-wide shipping, as well as guides for everyone weather or not you are a beginner or a skilled maker!

All of our guides are submitted by the community and we are always looking to expand our liberary! If your guide passes our quality check, you will even be compensated with discounts, gift cards and more!
